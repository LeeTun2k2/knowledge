August 2023 - Ko Samui, Thailand.

I went there on a family vacation and was lucky to stay at an extremely beautiful place.

But I couldn’t write long-form system design articles on my website as the internet and power at my place weren’t reliable.

So I decided to start this newsletter with short-form content and published the first article without a real plan.

Now I need your feedback, so I can help you better.
