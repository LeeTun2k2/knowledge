**[YouTube Scalability](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w5WVu624fY8)** (video, free)

The presentation explores the early-stage architecture of YouTube. It delves into how YouTube handles its massive user base and the intricate technical components that allow seamless video streaming and content delivery. The video provides insights into the challenges and solutions that contribute to YouTube's impressive scalability.

**[Timelines at Scale](https://www.infoq.com/presentations/Twitter-Timeline-Scalability/)** (video, free)

The presentation covers topics related to the scalability challenges and solutions involved in managing Twitter's timeline feature. It also includes discussions about how Twitter handles massive amounts of data and ensures a seamless user experience in the face of high traffic and data demands.

**[TripAdvisor Architecture](http://highscalability.com/blog/2011/6/27/tripadvisor-architecture-40m-visitors-200m-dynamic-page-view.html)** (article, free)

The article discusses the technical architecture and scalability challenges faced by TripAdvisor in handling the substantial data demands from 40 million visitors and 200 million dynamic page views.
