**[The Stack That Helped Medium Drive 2.6 Millennia of Reading Time](https://medium.engineering/the-stack-that-helped-medium-drive-2-6-millennia-of-reading-time-e56801f7c492)** (article, free)

Medium's success in driving over 2.6 millennia of reading time was fueled by its robust tech stack, empowering seamless content delivery and personalized experiences. By leveraging cutting-edge technologies and scalable infrastructure, Medium created a platform that captivated readers and enabled significant engagement with their content.

**[Designing Data-Intensive Applications: The Big Ideas Behind Reliable, Scalable, and Maintainable Systems](https://dataintensive.net/) **(book, paid)

Designing Data-Intensive Applications is a must-read book that provides a comprehensive guide to building robust and scalable data systems, emphasizing reliability and maintainability. With insightful explanations and real-world examples, this book equips developers and architects with the knowledge and principles needed to design data-intensive applications that can handle the challenges of modern data processing.

**[GEMS: Gossip-Enabled Monitoring Service for Scalable Heterogeneous Distributed Systems](https://citeseerx.ist.psu.edu/doc/10.1.1.160.2604)** (white paper, free)

The GEMS white paper introduces a novel monitoring service for scalable heterogeneous distributed systems, leveraging gossip-based communication to efficiently collect and disseminate monitoring data. By combining gossip protocols and adaptive sampling techniques, GEMS enables effective monitoring in large-scale distributed systems, enhancing scalability and accommodating diverse system components.
