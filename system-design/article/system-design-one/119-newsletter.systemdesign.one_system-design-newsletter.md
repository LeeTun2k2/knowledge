Thanks to the **1,700+ subscribers** of this newsletter - I have some **special news for you**. 

You will receive _simplified_ system design case studies every Sunday starting next week. 

_How is it helpful for you?_ The best method to improve your system design skills is to learn from real-world case studies.

The curated resources that you used to receive every week will be sent out only once a month along with the feed of articles that get published on the website. This will keep 100% signal and 0% noise in your inbox.

**I want to give you the best system design newsletter in this world.**
